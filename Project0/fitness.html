Inclined Dumbbell Curl

Follow the below steps:

  • Take two dumbbells and sit on a incline(30-45 degree) bench with your reet on the floor.
  • Moving only your forearms, slowly curl the dumbbells up to shoulder level. Keep your elbows pressed against your sides throughout the exercise.
  • Hold this position for a moment and sloWly lower the dumbbells. Make sure that only your forearms are moving

Seated Rows

Follow the steps below

  • Lie on your back with knees bent.
  • place your right foot on your left knee and raise your upper body towards the right knee.
  • Your left hand should be placed on the back of your head.
  • Bring back your head down slowly. repeat this with alternate position with left leg placed on right knee.

Parallel Bar Dips

Follow the below steps

  • Keep your head down during the course of the movement and move your body Torward.
  • Move your elbows Out a bit. That is, keep your elbows Out and away from your body.
  • This will ensure that you are using more chest than triceps. Remember, really concentrate on squeezing your entire chest area at the top of the movement
