Barbell Bicep curl

  • Inhale, then curl the barbell
  • Contract the gliteal, abdominal and back musces isometrically to avoid torso swing
  • Exhale as you complete the movement

Seated Rows

Follow the steps below

  • Sit down on the machine and place your feet on the front platform or crossbar provided making sure that your knees are slightly bent and not locked.
  • Lean over as you keep the natural alignment of your back and grab the V-bar handles.
  • With your arms extended pull back until your torso is at a 90-degree angle from your legs.

Barbell Squat

Follow the below steps

  • Step into the rack and position the bar at back of shoulders, neck height, grasping the bar on the SIdes. Pushing elbows up maintains bar stability and comfort.
  • Making sure to keep the chest pulled up, dismount from the rack and poSition heels shoulder width apart, facing 45 degrees out.
  • Descend to parallel or past for full rang motion and then push up through your heels, driving through your hips and Keeping your chest up. Repeat
